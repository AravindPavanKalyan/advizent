About Project

This scan-to-BIM project in North Carolina involved converting point cloud scan data into a comprehensive 3D BIM model for a multi-storey residential building. This project highlighted MaRS BIM Solutions’ ability to transform complex scan data into accurate and detailed BIM models, crucial for efficient planning, renovation, and management of multi-storey residential properties. Our expertise ensured that the provided scan data was meticulously converted into a reliable and detailed 3D model.

Our Scan to BIM services played a key role in streamlining the project by enabling precise visualization and analysis of the building’s structure. The generated BIM model helped in identifying potential design challenges early, ensuring smooth collaboration among stakeholders and facilitating accurate decision-making. By leveraging cutting-edge tools and proven workflows, we delivered a high-quality model that serves as a valuable resource for both current and future project phases.
